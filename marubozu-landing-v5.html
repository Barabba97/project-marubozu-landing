How it works

From idea to insight
in one workflow.

01

Log the trade

Enter pair, direction, entry, SL and TP. Link it to an exchange account. Balance allocation is tracked automatically.

02

Journal the process

Fill in your checklist, tag your timeframes, track your mood, write your thesis in Pre/During/Post sections. Build your trading memory.

03

Close with context

Record exit, PnL, fees and close reason. The macro context at the time of close will be snapshotted automatically — so you can always look back and know what the market was doing when you exited.

04

Review and improve

The dashboard surfaces your patterns. Where do you win? When do you break rules? The data tells you what your gut won't.
